Gamemodes

2 min

Everyone has 2 minutes to hit as many glowing buttons as possible.
Every time a new record is set the score will be saved in the leaderboard.

Simon says

There will be a automaticly generated sequence of glowing buttons.
And you have te press the buttons in the right order within the time frame.
Every time you complete the sequence the sequence will get longer.
And this will go on until you fail.

Speedrun

You have to hit a set amount of glowing buttons as fast as possible.
Every time a new record is hit the score will be saved in the leaderboard.

Shuttle run

Once the game starts, you will have a set amount of time to hit the glowing button.
Every time you hit the button in time, the time decreases.
If you hit the button too late, the game is over.
Every time a new record is hit the score will be saved in the leaderboard.
